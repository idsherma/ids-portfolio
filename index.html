About Me

Who am I?

Although my career started in television production, I believed that my love of journalism and digital media could be used together to create compelling news stories for the digital age. As a result, I learned how to create beautiful interactive experiences with code by using the latest front-end technologies. Essentially, I am a rare blend of the creative and the technical.

I am an alumnus of the S. I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University graduating with a Bachelors of Science with a major in TV, Radio, & Film. Originally from Washington DC but now residing in New York City, I'm looking to grow personally and professionally.

I want to be involved in projects where I get to work with an amazing team, create compelling user-facing applications while also advancing my development career. Take time to look at my work and if I can make your ideas a reality, get in touch.

Services

Web development

I bring a designer's vision to life by building websites and data visualizations to pixel perfection using HTML5, SASS, JavaScript, D3.js, Node.js, & React. From time to time, I get my hands dirty data mining using Python or with some back-end (Express.js, SQL).

Mobile First
